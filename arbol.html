Arbol Chillies with Fennel, Cumin and Honegar - 190ml

The Chilli de árbol is a small and potent chilli from Mexico. I have infused this chilli salsa with a dash of honegar - honegar was apparently use by the Romans as a cure-all. It is a mixture of honey and apple cider vinegar.

Great as a base for cooking or can be used as a condiment to spice up any dish including Italian, Chinese, Thai and Indian dishes.

Scorchometer: 7/10

To enjoy use the salsa in the following ways: 1. Simply add a teaspoon or more of salsa to any meal to add an intense hot chilli heat and warm rich flavour with an occasional tang of fennel, cumin, ginger and coriander. 2. Spice up cold cuts of cooked meat and serve it on the side. 3. Serve it with cheese and crackers. 4. Great Hors-d’oeuvre idea – rub garlic onto warm bruschetta, spread thinly with chilli salsa, fresh basil topped with mozzarella and pop under the grill until it’s melted. 5. Either Salsa is a perfect accompaniment for a chargrilled Steak or Hamburger. 6. Either Salsa can be used as a base for spicing up vegetables, meat or fish. 7. Recipe idea; Rub 2 – 3 teaspoons into a whole chicken approx. 20 mins before the chicken is completely cooked to enhance the flavour. 8. Perfect for brushing on meat whilst cooking on the BBQ.
