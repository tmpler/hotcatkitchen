Spicy Roast Chicken

How to spice Roast Chicken in a few simple steps:

  • Pop a raw Chicken into the oven with nothing else but a little salt and olive oil - roast in oven until nearly ready
  • Open Hot Cat Chilli Salsa
  • Add a tablespoon or two (depending on how hot you like it) to the Roast Chicken - Do this 5 mins before it's completely cooked Coat the Chicken in Salsa, spreading all over.
  • Pop back in the oven for 5-7 minutes.
  • After taking out of the oven - let the Chicken rest for 5 minutes
  • Slice and coat the pieces with the natural chicken salsa gravy
